ToL Scientific Core Contributor Registration

ToL People Registration

This page lets you register other biologists as ToL scientific core contributors. When you submit this registration form, our system will send a message to the registrant with information on how to get started as a ToL contributor. If you know what licenses your collaborator prefers for their text and media contributions, you can edit their default licenses after submitting the registration. If you are not sure, you can leave the default licenses in place. The registration message will contain information on how they can review and edit their license preferences themselves.

As always, if you have any questions or concerns, contact treegrow@tolweb.org.

Required information is marked with an asterisk*

On this page, you can register as someone interested in a particular group on the Tree of Life. You can register for any number of groups you care about. Once you have registered as an enthusiast for a given group, you will be listed on the ToL People page for that group. The information you enter in this form will be shown on your personal profile page on the ToL web site, so other people with similar interests will be able to learn more about you and contact you if you provide an email or homepage address.
Somebody has already registered with the ToL using that email address. If you need help updating your account, contact treegrow@tolweb.org. If you would like a new password to be emailed to that address, please click here.

You can make group assignments within the groups for which you have editing privileges. When you assign a group to a contributor, this person will automatically be given editing privileges for this group. You can also make group assignments by adding a registered contributor as an author to a ToL branch or leaf page.

Authors of branch pages generally coordinate the development of pages for subgroups. Uncheck The contributor will coordinate ... if the person will not participate in the coordination of the branch. If they will coordinate only certain subgroups, list those groups in the Comments field.

Phone and fax numbers will not be published. We request this information for administrative purposes only.

You may add as many groups as you want. Adding a group causes you to be listed on a page of all people who are interested in that group.
