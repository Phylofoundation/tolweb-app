Save and Preview Your Treehouse

After you click the "Save and Preview" button a preview of your treehouse will open in this window. Click "return to editing" at the top of your treehouse preview to get back to this form.

Once the current version of your work has been saved, you can close this window and come back whenever you are ready to add more information.
