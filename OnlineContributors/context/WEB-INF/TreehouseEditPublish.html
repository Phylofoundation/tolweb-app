    Editing tip:
    Publish

    When you are satisfied with your page you should submit it for publication on the ToL web site. Visitors to the ToL will be able to access your treehouse from the treehouse menu of the ToL branch or leaf page to which you attached your treehouse. Once you submit your treehouse for publication you will not be able to edit it until it has been approved for publication. If there is anything you are unsure about leave a note for one of the ToL editors in the notes section.
