Here's how you can make sure your data are saved to the ToL database:

  • Data are saved if you go to another form by clicking on a link in the sidebar above.
  • Data are saved whenever you click on the View Page button above.
  • If you want to leave this window without going through View Page, you can click on the Save button below to make sure your data have been committed to the database.

Notes

Notes are not printed on web pages. You can use this space to record notes for yourself or for your co-authors.

 

Use View Page to save the information entered above to the ToL database and to switch to the window with the updated version of the page you are editing. This preview is located on the ToL development server, which is password protected, so it can be accessed only by ToL contributors and their collaborators.

If you click on Save, the data in this form will be saved to the ToL database, and you can move to another window in your browser without having to worry about losing any of your changes.

When you are ready to release one or more pages on the public version of the ToL site, click on Publication Manager. This will take you to a form that let's you select the pages you want to have published.
