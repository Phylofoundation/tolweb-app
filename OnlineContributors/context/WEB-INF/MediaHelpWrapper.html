Save Your Data!

In order to save the data entered in the Edit Data form to the ToL database, you need to click on the Submit Data button at the bottom of the form.

Don't close the form without submitting your data first. Also, your connection to the ToL database will time out in about an hour. If it takes you longer to finish the form, you should submit an incomplete version first, and then start another session to revise and expand your entries.
